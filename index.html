LoneMartian - Stream Rules

Before participating in the chat, please make yourself familiar with the rules listed here, for the best experience.

  1. link Refrain from discussing sensitive topics

    Don't be a dick and use common sense.

    This means avoid talking about sensitive topics, such as politics, religion, and anything else that would cause arguments and make the chat unwelcoming overall.

  2. link Treat others with respect

    Don't start fights with one another and treat others how you'd expect them to treat you back.

    Any form of hate speech will NOT be tolerated.

  3. link No redirecting to other chatrooms

    Please don't post invites to external chatrooms, such as Discord.

  4. link No announcing

    If another YouTuber either uploads a video or starts streaming, be polite and don't announce it in the chat.

  5. link No NSFW

    Do not link gore, pornography or other graphic material in the chat. Keep it to yourself.
    This also means no posting sex toys or anything similar.

  6. link No spoilers or backseating

    When I ask to not post spoilers, please respect that, otherwise you'll ruin it for me and others watching the stream.

  7. link Don't spam

    ASCII art or copypastas will NOT be tolerated and will result in a mute.

    Also don't post long multi-emote chains either.

  8. link No mirroring to other sites

    Please don't mirror the streams to other sites, such as Twitch.

  9. link Follow YouTube's Community Guidelines

    To view the guidelines, please see https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/9288567.

Please be aware that these rules are subject to change at anytime.
