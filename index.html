LoneMartian - Stream Rules

Please make yourself familiar with the rules before participating. Thank you.

  • 1. Follow YouTube's Community Guidelines

    Please see https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/9288567 for more information.

  • 2. Keep conversations in English.

    This helps with making moderation easier and allowing the streamer to read chat messages.

    So try to speak English as best as you can or use an online translator.

  • 3. No NSFW.

    No discussing or linking gore, pornography and any explicit material in chat. Keep that to yourself.

    This also means no posting or linking to s*x toys or anything of that nature.

  • 4. No sensitive topics.

    Don't be a douchebag and use common sense.

    This also means no discussing topics that may cause trolling and hostility, such as mental health, politics, religion, etc.

  • 5. Be respectful.

    Pretty self-explanatory. Be chill and don't start fights in chat.

  • 6. No linking to other content creators, websites, etc.

    If another content creator posts something or starts livestreaming, then please be polite and do not announce it in the chat.

    Don't post links or invites to social media profiles (including YouTube channels), chatrooms (such as Discord), websites, etc.

  • 7. No posting spoilers in chat.

    Pretty self-explanatory. Don't post spoilers from any media in the chat, otherwise you'll ruin it for me and the people watching the stream.

  • 8. No spamming.

    ASCII art and copypastas are NOT tolerated.

    No spamming the same phrase over and over in chat.

    Refrain from posting long multi-emote strings.
